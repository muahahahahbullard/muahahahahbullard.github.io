It is no secret that our sun is getting older. It happens to everyone, and celestial bodies are no exception. We at Jet3 like to assure everyone who stays with us that our sun's looming expansion serves us no worry. Our wonderful Europa will pick up a tropical tone, with beautiful red skies, 85 hours a day. Our facilities are built to withstand a shift in temperature, and with a boat in every hallway, you can rest assured knowing that we'll continue to float your boat long after our ice melts away.


Jet3 would like to remind its valued guests that once the planet is dry, all travel packages will be voided and the planet must be vacated. Those working on-world will be permitted to stay under the stipulation that they are outfitted and ready by the time the work horn blasts.

The Cabin.

You're on a path in the woods. And at the end of that path is a cabin. And in the basement of that cabin is a princess. You're here to slay her. If you don't, it will be the end of the world.
You step inside the cabin. A pristine blade lies on a table to your left. Short, Triangular, Reliable. On the right, there is a mirror, coated in a thin film of dust, yet the glimmer of the starlit night reflects through the smooth glass.
And right in front of you, is a basement. it has a long, winding hallway down, unfinished, like a mineshaft, and smelling of earth and old, dryrotted wood.


You have been left with 3 choices, prior to you meeting the princess
  • You can grab the blade, then make your decent
  • You can make your decent unarmed
  • You can turn around, and hope the burden of taking a life is laid on another's hand

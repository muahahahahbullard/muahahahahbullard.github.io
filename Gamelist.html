5 games of all time

#1 UNDERTALE 10/10, Story and soundtrack, it comes with a pretty cool game too, the story is that youre a human who fell from the surface, and you can either spare monsters, or kill them.
#2 ONESHOT 9.5/10, Amazing story and gameplay, i HIGHLY recommend, its a meta-game that has puzzles that take you through your computer, and ties the world of the game to your computer, or "the world machine"
#3 Hollow Knight 9.5/10, Beautiful story that you have to figure out rather than be spoonfed, challenging and fun combat, and amazing character interactions
#4 Your Only Move is Hustle a.k.a YOMI 9.2/10, Amazing game, a turn-based side scrolling fighting game that plays like chess does intellectually. it has an amazing modding community, and youre bound to find a mod with YOUr favorite characters!!
#5 DELTARUNE 8.9/10, Amazing meta narrative, and wonderful cast of diverse, and silly characters, every experience in this game is NEVER GLOOBY!!

Europan Attractions: Underwater zoo and aquarium

Jet3 Tours is proud to present a limited time offer. Guests of the Europan Orbital can enjoy free admission to the Undersea Zoo and Aquarium. Their zoo features all of your favorite animals from earth, and will be opening their butterfly pavillion this Sunday. The aquarium is home to over 1,000 species of saltwater and freshwater fish, and guests can even try their hand at authentic Europan ice fishing.

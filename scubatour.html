Feel the weight of an ocean.

Bundled in your Europan getaway is an all-inclusive scuba diving tour into Europa's beautiful frozen ocean. Experience the sensation of the sub-zero waters as you take the plunge into the heart of the planet, and dive up to 700 feet, largely in part to the lower gravity on our ocean moon. Take a dive with our unique breed of Europan Cavern fish as you take our universally recognized cave-diving class, or take part in our underwater scavenger hunt for a chance to win the covetous Diver's Cup.

Please be aware that a signed waiver is required before any person or persons enters the water. Experienced divers may think they are fit to dive deeper than the reccomended 700 ft. limit. They are mistaken.
